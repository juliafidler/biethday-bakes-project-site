About

A small idea with a personal purpose.

Birthday Bakes was created to make sure children in local programs still get to feel celebrated. The goal is simple: provide a cake, a sweet moment, and a memory that feels personal.

Who can request

Organization staff, case workers, shelter coordinators, and approved program contacts.

What is offered

Personalized cakes or cupcakes based on availability, timing, and dietary restrictions.

How it starts

Requests are submitted ahead of time and coordinated directly with the organization.

Service area

Rochester, Rochester Hills, Pontiac, and nearby communities.

How it works

Simple, request-based, and easy to coordinate.

1. A staff member reaches out

A partner organization submits a request for a child’s birthday celebration.

2. Details are confirmed

Age, date needed, allergies, and whether a cake or cupcakes would be best.

3. Pickup or drop-off is arranged

Everything is coordinated in the safest and easiest way for the organization.

Requests

Request a birthday cake

Requests should ideally be submitted at least 1 to 2 weeks in advance. Since Birthday Bakes is still growing, availability may be limited each month.

Suggested request details
  • Child's first name or initials
  • Age turning
  • Date needed
  • Allergies or restrictions
  • Cake or cupcakes
  • Colors or simple theme
